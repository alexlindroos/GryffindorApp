Gryffindoge is a media sharing application that can be used for sharing images, videos and audio. It’s a web based application firstly designed for mobile use. People who are commuting and travelling want to have something to pass time and browsing funny images or videos is great way to do so. Some people also like to listen to podcasts while they commute and that’s why Gryffindoge offers audio as a category.
It is made for people who live active lifestyle and want to share events to others. Social events like student parties bring people together and by posting various images or videos from these parties people can feel that they are part of something.
